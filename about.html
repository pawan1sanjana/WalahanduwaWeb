"At Walahanduwa Tea Factory, a proud member of the Evergreen Group—located in the picturesque coastal town of Galle, Sri Lanka—we specialize in crafting the finest Ceylon tea with a blend of traditional craftsmanship and modern techniques."

Our Legacy

Situated just a stone's throw from the sea, Walahanduwa Tea Factory has been producing premium Ceylon tea for decades. Our factory, part of the prestigious Evergreen Group, combines time-honored tea-making traditions with sustainable practices to create exceptional blends that reflect the region’s unique coastal environment.

From hand-picked leaves to precision processing, every step of our tea-making process is designed to deliver the finest flavor in every cup. With a deep commitment to quality and sustainability, we take pride in creating a product that not only delights the senses but also supports our local community and the environment.

"A blend of heritage, innovation, and sustainability in every cup."
