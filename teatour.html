Walahanduwa Tea Factory Tour – Galle

Established in the heart of Sri Lanka’s southern tea region, Walahanduwa Tea Factory is a historic tea-producing facility renowned for its premium-quality Ceylon tea. Located near the coastal city of Galle, the factory blends traditional and modern techniques to craft exceptional teas.

Visitors can explore the intricate process of tea production, from plucking to withering, rolling, fermenting, drying, and grading. Surrounded by lush tea gardens, the factory offers guided tours, tea tasting sessions, and insights into sustainable tea production.

  • Guided tour of a working tea factory
